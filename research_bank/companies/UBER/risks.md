# UBER Risks

## Key risks

1. Regulatory and labor classification risk.
2. Competitive intensity across mobility and delivery.
3. Overinterpreting Delivery as proof of high-quality economics.
4. Operational and organizational complexity.
5. Valuation risk.
6. AV disintermediation risk if demand ownership moves away from Uber.

## Current risk focus

The most important current analytical risk is confusing strong platform activity with high-quality monetization. Q1 2026 suggested strong bookings/trips and strong Delivery, but uneven revenue conversion in Mobility.
