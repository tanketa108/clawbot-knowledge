# Marketplace Frameworks

Reusable analytical frameworks for marketplace businesses.

## Core dimensions

- Demand control
- Supply liquidity
- Matching quality
- Frequency and retention
- Take rate sustainability
- Incentive dependence
- Local versus global network effects
- Trust, safety and reliability
- Regulatory exposure

## Default caution

Marketplace scale is not automatically a moat. The moat must appear in observable user outcomes, supplier economics, lower acquisition cost, pricing power, margins or retention.
