# Unit Economics — Mobility & Delivery

## Mobility economics

Primary drivers: gross bookings, take rate, incentives, insurance/regulatory costs, driver supply balance, pricing power and utilization.

## Delivery economics

Primary drivers: order frequency, average order value, merchant commissions, delivery fees, courier incentives, batching efficiency, advertising attach rate and subscription subsidies.

## Analytical caution

Do not infer attractive economics from growth alone. Require evidence that growth converts into durable margin, cash flow, or strategic value across the ecosystem.
