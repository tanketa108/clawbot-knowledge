# Business Models — Mobility & Delivery

## Mobility

Two-sided local marketplace matching riders with drivers. Economics depend on local liquidity, pricing, utilization, incentives, insurance/regulatory costs, and platform take rate.

## Delivery

Three-sided marketplace connecting consumers, merchants and couriers. Strategically powerful for frequency and engagement, but often structurally more competitive and margin-constrained than mobility.

## Membership

Subscription layer designed to increase frequency, retention and cross-product usage. Must be tested for true incremental value versus subsidized activity that would have happened anyway.

## Advertising

High-margin monetization layer if consumer engagement and merchant demand are sufficient. Requires careful separation from core marketplace take rate.
