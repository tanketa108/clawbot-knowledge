# Mobility & Delivery Sector Research Backlog

## Priority 1 — Comparable company map

Map Uber, Lyft, DoorDash, Deliveroo, Grab, DiDi, Delivery Hero and Just Eat Takeaway across:

- core geography,
- mobility vs delivery mix,
- take rate,
- contribution/EBITDA margin,
- membership ecosystem,
- ads exposure,
- regulatory profile.

## Priority 2 — Local density evidence framework

Define what evidence proves local density advantage:

- ETA advantage,
- availability advantage,
- lower incentives,
- higher conversion,
- retention/frequency,
- stable or expanding take rate,
- share gains without promotional intensity.

## Priority 3 — Membership economics framework

Create reusable framework for subscription/membership programs in local marketplaces:

- gross adds vs retained members,
- frequency uplift,
- cross-product usage,
- subsidy cost,
- net contribution margin impact,
- churn and cohort behavior.

## Priority 4 — Delivery profitability framework

Define when delivery is a standalone quality business versus a strategic engagement layer.

## Priority 5 — AV value-chain map

Map possible AV industry structures and who captures value: demand platform, AV tech provider, fleet owner, OEM, insurer, regulator/local operator.
