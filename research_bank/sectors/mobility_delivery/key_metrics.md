# Key Metrics — Mobility & Delivery

## Demand and activity

- Gross bookings / GOV
- Trips / orders
- Monthly active platform consumers
- Frequency per active user
- Retention and cohort behavior
- Membership penetration

## Marketplace quality

- ETA / wait time
- Availability by geography and time of day
- Fulfillment reliability
- Cancellation rates
- Driver/courier supply liquidity

## Monetization

- Revenue
- Take rate
- Advertising revenue
- Subscription revenue
- Cross-sell between products

## Profitability

- Contribution profit / margin
- Adjusted EBITDA by segment
- Incentives and promotions
- Insurance / regulatory cost burden
- Corporate overhead allocation

## Strategic quality

- Local market share
- Competitive intensity
- Regulatory exposure
- Product breadth
- Control of customer relationship
