# Workflow — New Company Research

Before creating a new company thesis, Clawbot must avoid duplicating existing sector knowledge.

## Steps

1. Identify sector and relevant frameworks.
2. Read the sector bank.
3. Read comparable company files if available.
4. Create company folder from template.
5. Write only company-specific deltas.
6. Add reusable insights back to sector files.
7. Add entries to indexes.
8. Record material thesis decisions in `changelog.md`.

## Acceptance criteria

- No duplicated sector primer inside company files.
- Company thesis links to sector context.
- Open questions are separated into sector-level and company-level.
- Evidence log contains source-backed items.
